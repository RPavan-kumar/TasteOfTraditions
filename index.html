OUR AUTHENTICITY

Cooking is the process of producing safe and edible food by preparing and combining ingredients, and (in most cases) applying heat. Cooking is a means of processing food, without which many foods would be unfit for human consumption

Traditional foods are foods and dishes that are passed on through generations or which have been consumed for many generations. Traditional foods and dishes are traditional in nature, and may have a historic precedent in a national dish, regional cuisine or local cuisine. Traditional foods and beverages may be produced as homemade, by restaurants and small manufacturers, and by large food processing plant facilities.

In Telangana, every dish tells a story with the ingredients used in a recipe. It includes various local spices, agricultural produce and more, adding layers and texture to our everyday meal. The best part is, we find an extensive range of foods and beverages in the cuisine- each of which has a unique character, making it stand out in the lot. What contributes to the versatility is the cooking technique. If you explore, you will find Indians using different cooking methods for different dishes. Besides the ingredients, technique too plays a major role in defining the character of a dish. In this article, we will explore some of the traditional cooking techniques that are widely used in Indian cuisine.
