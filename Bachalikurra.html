Bachali Kurra

Ingredients:

  • Bachali leaves (Malabar Spinach) - 2 cups (cleaned and chopped)
  • Toor dal (pigeon pea lentils) - 1/2 cup
  • Green chilies - 2 to 3 (slit lengthwise)
  • Onion - 1 medium (chopped)
  • Tomato - 1 large (chopped)
  • Tamarind - lemon-sized ball (soaked in water)
  • Garlic cloves - 2 to 3 (crushed)
  • Mustard seeds - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Cumin seeds - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Dried red chilies - 2
  • Curry leaves - a few
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Red chili powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Salt - to taste
  • Water - as required

Recipe:

  • Wash and soak toor dal in water for about 30 minutes.
  • In a pressure cooker, add soaked toor dal, chopped bachali leaves, chopped onion, chopped tomato, slit green chilies, crushed garlic, turmeric powder, red chili powder, tamarind, and salt to taste.
  • Add water as required and pressure cook for about 3-4 whistles or until the dal is cooked well.
  • Once the pressure releases, heat oil in a separate pan for tempering.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Then add cumin seeds, dried red chilies, and curry leaves, saut√© for a few seconds.
  • Add the cooked dal mixture to the tempering and mix well.
  • Simmer for a few minutes until the flavors blend together.
  • Adjust the consistency by adding more water if required.
  • Bachali Kura is ready to serve. Serve hot with rice or roti.
  • Enjoy the nutritious and flavorful Bachali Kura as a wholesome meal!
